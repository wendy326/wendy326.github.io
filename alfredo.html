WENDY SUN

Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter (4 tbsp)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • (optional) chicken
  • (optional) broccoli

Directions

  1. In a skillet, melt butter, then saute garlic on low heat until golden
  2. Slowly and gradually stir in flour until dissolved and smooth
  3. Gradually stir in parmesan until it melts and thickens
  4. Slowly pour in milk and mix for ~5 minutes
  5. Season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper
  6. Continue to cook and stir until sauce reaches desired consistency
  7. Toss in noodles and other add-ins

Italian seasoning: 2 tbsp dried basil, 2 tbsp dried oregano, 2 tbsp dried parsley, 1 tbsp dried rosemary, 1 tbsp dried thyme, 1 tbsp red chili flakes, 1 tsp garlic powder. Fresh ingredients work as substitutes for the dried ones, just remember that you need more of each because the dried ones are much more concentrated. Parmesan cheese: 2 parts Walmart-brand parmesan plus 1 part milk, heated and mixed together.
