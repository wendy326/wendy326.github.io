Banana Bread Muffins

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cups granulated sugar (maybe a bit less)
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 mashed ripe bananas (2/3 cup)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 2/3 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • (optional) cinnamon sugar topping

Directions

  1. In medium bowl, whisk together the sugar, oil, eggs
  2. Whisk in bananas and vanilla until combined
  3. Sift in flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon and stir together until just combined
  4. Heat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit and prep 12 regular sized muffin cups
  5. Divide batter evenly among muffin cups (about 3/4 full)
  6. Bake 23-24 minutes or until the muffins pass the toothpick test and their tops appear high, rounded, and not wet
  7. Remove and let cool, then add optional cinnamon sugar topping
