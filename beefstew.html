WENDY SUN

Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2.5 lb beef
  • ~3 tbsp soy sauce
  • ~1/2 tbsp Chinese vinegar
  • ~3 oz Velveeta cheese
  • ~2 tbsp sugar
  • water
  • (optional) hawthorn

Directions

  1. Soften the beef by pounding it with a knife and making numerous small slits
  2. Cut the beef into ~2 inch chunks
  3. Put meat into a large pot and fill pot up with water until the water is level with the meat
  4. Boil
  5. Scrape blood foam from top of the water
  6. Dice onion and add to pot
  7. Add ~3 tbsp soy sauce, or until water is light brown
  8. Add Chinese vinegar
  9. Add sugar and hawthorn
  10. Add Velveeta
  11. Let boil with lid partially on, until the water covers half the meat and the onion has dissolved
  12. Serve over rice or with bread

You can replace the beef with pork, just chop the porkin into 3 inch chunks (Step 2). If you want a thicker sauce, boil for longer at the end, or if you want more of a soup, boil for less time.
