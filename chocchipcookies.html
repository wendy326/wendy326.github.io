Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks salted butter at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 12 oz semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together flour, baking soda, and salt
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter, white and brown sugar, and vanilla extract until light and fluffy
  3. Add in eggs one at a time to the butter mixture, and mix thoroughly
  4. Slowly add in the dry ingredients until well combined
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  7. Scoop 2 tbsp balls of dough and set them 2 inches apart on a baking tray lined with parchment paper
  8. Gently press the dough
  9. Bake for ~13 minutes, or until the cookies are brown

Makes ~36 cookies. Keep a close eye on the cookies while they're baking. Make sure the centers are fully cooked.
