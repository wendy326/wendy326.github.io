Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs at room temperature
  • 4 (to 5) cups flour (added when kneading)
  • 2 1/2 tsp yeast

Filling

  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup softened butter

Icing

  • 1 stick softened butter
  • 1/4 cup softened cream cheese
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Grease an 8x11 inch or 9x13 inch baking dish
  2. In a small bowl, stir together the milk, yeast, and sugar, then set aside for 5 minutes or until the yeast is foamy
  3. Dough - In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, salt, and eggs
  4. Stir in the yeast mixture until combined
  5. Add flour one cup at a time (the mixture will be sticky)
  6. Use hands to roughly knead the mixture, then turn it out onto a floured surface
  7. Knead for 4-5 minutes until smooth, sprinkling with more flour if needed, and form into a ball
  8. Brush a large bowl with oil and place dough inside, then cover with plastic wrap and set aside in a warm place to rise until dounled in size (~1 hour)
  9. Filling - mix brown sugar, cinnamon, and softened butter together
  10. Punch down dough and roll out (~1/4 inch) on a floured surface into a 20x14 inch rectangle
  11. Spread filling to within 1/4 inches of the edges of the dough
  12. Starting at one of the shortest 14 inch ends, roll tightly into a log, then slice into rolls
  13. Place rolls into the greased baking dish cut-side up, cover, then let rise for 1 hour
  14. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Farenheit
  15. Brush the tops of the rolls with butter
  16. Bake cinnamon rolls in the oven for 25-30 minutes
  17. Cool slightly and ice
