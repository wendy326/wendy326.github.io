WENDY SUN

Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder (maybe a bit less?)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Soak cornmeal in milk for 10-15 minutes
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degress Farenheit
  3. Oil a muffin tin or a 9 inch round cake pan
  4. Combine all the ingredients
  5. For muffins: bake for 18 minutes
  6. For loaf: bake for 20-25 minutes
  7. Cornbread should pass the toothpick test

brief description/tips - pay close attention to the center of loaf, it takes longer to bake
