WENDY SUN

Lemon Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened (16 tbsp)
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tbsp lemon zest
  • 4 eggs

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a large bowl, combine butter, 2 cups of flour, and 1/2 a cup of sugar
  3. Press mixture to the bottom of an ungreased 9x13 inch pan, making the center thinner and the edges thicker
  4. Use a fork to poke holes into the crust
  5. Bake for 13-20 minutes, or until crust is golden brown
  6. In another bowl, whisk together 1 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup flour, eggs, lemon juice, and lemon zest
  7. Pour over the baked crust
  8. Bake for 20 minutes and let cool

You can microwave the lemons for ~15 seconds to make them more juicy. Also, you can roll them. Can also add 1/4 tsp salt to the crust.
