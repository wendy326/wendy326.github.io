WENDY SUN

Lime Cake

Ingredients

    Cake
    • 2 1/2 cups cake flour
    • 1 1/2 cups sugar
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
    • 1 1/2 (12 tbsp) unsalted butter, soft and cold
    • 4 large eggs, room temp
    • 1 cup milk
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1/4 cup lime juice
    • zest of 2 limes
    Lime Simple Syrup
    • 1/2 cup water
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1 tbsp lime juice
    Lime Curd
    • 3/8 (1/4 and 1/8) cups sugar
    • 1/8 cup cornstarch
    • 1/2 cup water
    • 1 egg yolk, slightly beaten
    • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
    • 1/2 tbsp lime zest
    • 2 tbsp lime juice

Directions

    Making the cake
    1. In a large bowl, combine the cake flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and lime zest.
    2. In a small bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and lime juice.
    3. Whisk the dry ingredients for 30 seconds to combine.
    4. Begin with mixer on low speed, gradually increasing to medium as you add the slices of butter. Beat until the dry ingredients are crumbly and moist. It will look/feel like coarse sand.
    5. Scrape the bottom and sides of bowl to make sure there isn't any dry flour.
    6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
    7. Grease 2 8x2 in round pans or a 8x13x2 in baking tray
    8. Slowly add half of the egg mixture to the dry ingredients and beat at medium speed for 1 1/2 minutes, the batter will become thick and fluffy.
    9. Scrape the bowl and add remaining egg mixture in 2 additions, beating for 20 seconds after each addition.
    10. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes (25 to 30 minutes if you bake in 3 pans) or until it passes toothpick test. Let cool 10 minutes then turn out.
    Making the simple syrup
    1. In a saucepan over medium heat bring the water and sugar to a boil.
    2. Turn the heat to low and stir constantly until the sugar dissolves and the mixture is clear.
    3. Cook a little longer over low heat to make the consistency thicker.
    4. Remove from heat and stir in the lime juice, then let cool.
    Making the curd
    1. Combine sugar and cornstarch in a saucepan, pour in water.
    2. Over medium heat, stir mixture constantly until it thickens and comes to a boil.
    3. Boil for 1 minute, remove the pan from the heat.
    4. Spoon about 1/4 cup of the hot mixture into a small bowl containing the egg yolk and stir quickly until combined.
    5. Pour egg mixture back into saucepan. Return pan to medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, for about 1 min. The mixture will thicken slightly.
    6. Remove from heat and stir in the butter, lime juice and lime zest. Let cool.
    Assembling the cake
    1. After everything has cooled, brush cake layers with lime simple syrup.
    2. Spread lime curd between each layer.
    3. Top with frosting of choice.

    Cake flour is just (1 cup - 2 tbsp) flour + 2 tbsp cornstarch sifted together twice. You can try using a bit more flour and less cornstarch as this ratio results in a pretty pasty cake. This recipe ends up using 4-5 limes (juice of all, zest of 3). Be cautious with the lime curd, it's pretty strong (aka don't cover the cake in curd like the picture does). When I baked the cake, I set a sheet of parchment paper on the bottom of the pan for easy removal later. Compared to the original recipe, I halved the amount of simple syrup and curd.
