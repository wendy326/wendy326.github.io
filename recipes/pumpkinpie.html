Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 oz) can pumpkin
  • 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp finely grated ginger
  • 1 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 (9 in) pie crust

Directions

  1. Whisk pumpkin, cinnamon, ginger, and salt on low heat, then let cool
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degress Farenheit
  3. Combine all the ingredients then pour into crust
  4. Bake for 15 minutes
  5. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees Farenheit and continue baking for 30 minutes or until it passes the toothpick test
  6. Turn off heat and leave pie in oven for 10-20 minutes
  7. Let cool

Let pie cool slowly in oven so it won't crack, extra emphasis on finely chopped/grated ginger, or else u can taste/feel the chunks
