WENDY SUN

Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tbsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve sugar in warm water, then stir in yeast. Allow to proof until yeast resembles a creamy foam (~10 min)
  2. Mix salt and oil into the yeast. Mix in flour one cup at a time.
  3. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth.
  4. Place in a well oiled bowl, and turn dough to coat, then cover with a damp cloth.
  5. Allow to rise until doubled in bulk (~1 hour)
  6. Punch dough down and knead for a few minutes
  7. Shape into a loaf, and place into a well oiled 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  8. Allow to rise for 30 minutes, or until dough has risen 1 inch above pans.
  9. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.

Added extra sugar and cinnamon compared to the original recipe. The vegetable oil isn't really necessary, the waffles are fine without so I just used a little bit, not the full half cup. Waffles freeze well, can microwave them for one minute for a quick breakfast.
