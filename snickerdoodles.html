WENDY SUN

Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter (2 sticks)
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar or lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp white sugar

Directions

  1. In medium bowl, cream together the butter and 1 1/2 cups sugar
  2. Stir in eggs
  3. Sift together flour, baking soda, salt, and cream of tartar, then stir into creamed mixture until well blended
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  5. In a small bowl, stir together the 2 tbsp sugar and cinnamon
  6. Roll dough into walnut-sized balls, then coat them in cinnamon sugar
  7. Place balls onto cookie sheet, 2 inches apart, then press gently with spatula
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until edges are slightly brown, then let cool on wire racks
